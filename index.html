Hello there!

Welcome one, welcome all! Imperial College Robotics Society is a student-led university club aiming to increase robotics interest at Imperial College London and across the UK. We run workshops, competitions and projects to teach about robotics, electronics and software as well as providing help (financial and technical) to students looking to start a robotics project.

What do we do

Robotics 101

We run robotics 101 , our flagship open source introductory robotics course, throughout the academic year.

Wednesday Hackdays

People work on various cool robotics projects on Wednesdays. Members usually provide technical help and support to one another's projects during these sessions.

Facilities for Makers

We have a soldering station, laser cutter and 3D printers for our members to build whatever they want.

External Competitions

We participate in external competitions such as piwars and eurobot.
